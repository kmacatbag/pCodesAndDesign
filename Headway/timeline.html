Late January 2019

Around late January, the project was given to us. This project had options whether to pick an organization or create your own which can be our hobbies. As you had explored, you can tell that this is one of the programmers hobbies: to design and be organized.

Early February 2019

An idea from two certain apps suddenly taken by surprised was used among the group of friends and decided to imagine if there would be a website that contains the same exact thing as the apps.

Late February 2019 - Early March 2019

Procastination took place in which the programmer forgot about the project but manage to do the other assignments that was due including the homeworks needing to be completed in other classes, in which everything was stressful.

Mid March 2019

Brainstorming to what the website should look like came into mind and began to explore different images and ways to put it into the first page of the website. Trial and error occured in which was natural.

Late March 2019

With everything that was happening from the other classees, the programmer finally squeezed some time to get started with the website. She got the pages done but was not pleasing in which she restarted from scratch.

April 1, 2019

Three days left until the project and the report are due. The programmer began to panic as she found how messy her code looked in which she restarted.

April 2, 2019

The programmer began to be stress but she used the app that inspired her to due this website in which helped her. The programmer was also able to ask her friends on what would they like to see on the website, in which inspired her even more and began to strive to built the website.

April 3, 2019

One day left as the programmer tries to finish her website and report done. She manages to finish the report and the website seeing that there was passion towards programming a website that she truly desired.

April 4, 2019

The programmer hands in the USB containing the website towards the professor. Having weights being lifted off her shoulders, she smiled but then remembers, assignment 3 is due on Saturday.
